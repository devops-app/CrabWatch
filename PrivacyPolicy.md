# Privacy Policy – CrabWatch Platform

## 1. Introduction

CrabWatch ("the Platform") is a mobile and web-based system developed to support biodiversity research and conservation through citizen science participation.

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, used, stored, and protected in connection with the Platform.

## 2. Data Controller

**Project Owner (Data Controller):**
Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT)

**Technical Service Provider (Data Processor):**
Dsigncodehub

UMT is responsible for the governance and ownership of all platform data, while Dsigncodehub provides infrastructure hosting and technical support.

## 3. Personal Data Collected

The Platform may collect the following categories of data:

### 3.1 Account Information

- Name
- Email address
- Login credentials (secured)

### 3.2 Observation Data

- Uploaded images
- Geolocation (GPS coordinates)
- Species information
- Measurement data (e.g., carapace length, sex)
- Submission timestamps

### 3.3 Technical Data

- Device type and OS
- Log and usage data
- IP address (where applicable)

## 4. Purpose of Data Processing

Data is collected and used for:

- User registration and authentication
- Submission and management of observations
- Scientific research and biodiversity analysis
- Platform operation, support, and improvement
- Communication with users (notifications, updates)

## 5. Legal Basis (PDPA Compliance)

Personal data is processed in accordance with:

- **Malaysia Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA)**

Processing is based on:

- User consent
- Legitimate project and research purposes

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your data may be shared with:

### 6.1 Authorised Parties

- Researchers and academic institutions
- Platform administrators
- Technical support personnel (Dsigncodehub)

### 6.2 Conditions of Sharing

- Only where necessary for research, validation, or system operation
- Subject to role-based access control
- In compliance with applicable laws

### 6.3 Legal Disclosure

Data may be disclosed if required by:

- Law enforcement agencies
- Legal or regulatory obligations

## 7. User-Generated Content

- Users retain ownership of their submitted content
- By submitting content, users grant a **non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free licence** for use in research, reporting, education, and conservation

Data may be:

- Aggregated
- Anonymised
- Published in research outputs

## 8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

- Authentication and access control
- Role-based permissions
- Secure data transmission
- System monitoring and logging
- Regular updates and security patching

However, no system is completely secure, and users acknowledge inherent risks.

## 9. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for:

- Research
- Operational
- Legal purposes

Data may be retained longer for:

- Scientific datasets
- Institutional research continuity

## 10. User Rights

Users may have the right to:

- Access their personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion (subject to research/legal constraints)
- Withdraw consent (where applicable)

Requests should be directed to the project owner (UMT).

## 11. Third-Party Services

The Platform may use third-party services such as:

- Map services
- Authentication services
- Cloud infrastructure providers
- AI services (if applicable)

These providers operate under their own privacy policies.

## 12. AI Processing (If Applicable)

Where AI technologies are used:

- Results are automatically generated
- Outputs may not always be accurate
- Users should not rely solely on AI-generated insights

## 13. International Data Transfer

If applicable, data may be processed in cloud environments located outside Malaysia.
All data handling will follow appropriate safeguards and security requirements.

## 14. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect:

- Legal changes
- Platform enhancements
- Operational updates

Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
